Based on the events transmitted to Google Analytics, you can generate the necessary reports in the future.</p>

	<p class="paragraph"><b>Parameters that are most frequently transmitted in events:</b></p>
	<ul>
		<li class="list-item">deal budget</li>
		<li class="list-item">reasons for loss</li>
		<li class="list-item">stage from which the loss occurred</li>
		<li class="list-item">stage to which the deal moved</li>
	</ul>
